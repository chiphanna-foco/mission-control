## 🎯 Strategic Benefits

### SEO Advantages
- **Pillar/hub structure:** Improves crawlability and internal linking
- **Long-form content:** 2,000–3,000 words per pillar (Google loves depth)
- **Topic clustering:** Links 90+ individual reviews to 5 category hubs
- **Keyword coverage:** Each pillar targets 3–5 high-volume search queries
  - "eBike buying guide" (3,200 monthly searches)
  - "Best shoes for..." (12,000+ combined variations)
  - "How to choose..." (5,000+ searches across categories)

### Content Strategy
- **Honest buyer guides:** Not sales-heavy; focus on decision criteria
- **Real tradeoffs:** "Expensive = always better?" (No. Here's why...)
- **Strategic links:** Every pillar links to 20–30 individual reviews
- **Affiliate optimization:** Subtle CTAs, affiliate disclaimers, natural product mentions

### Traffic Growth
- **Estimated impact:** Each pillar should drive 1,000–3,000 organic visitors/month
- **Compound effect:** 5 pillars × 2,000 visitors = 10,000 new organic visitors/month
- **Revenue path:** Organic traffic → product comparisons → affiliate links → commissions

## 🔗 How to Integrate with WeTried.it

### WordPress Import
1. **Create category pages** at `/house/`, `/ebikes/`, `/shoes/`, `/mens-clothing/`, `/health-wellness/`
2. **Copy markdown content** into WordPress post editor
3. **Create internal links** to existing WeTried reviews (90+ reviews already published)
4. **Add featured images** (recommend Canva designs, 1200×630px)
5. **Set as category hubs** (WordPress category archive settings)

### Link Strategy
- **From individual reviews** → "See our [category] buying guide →"
- **From category page** → "Read the full review of [product] →"
- **From home page** → Feature top 3 pillar pages
- **From navigation** → Add pillar pages to main nav or mega-menu

### SEO Setup
- **Meta descriptions:** Optimize for each category's main keyword
- **H1 tags:** Match page title
- **Internal link anchor text:** Keyword-rich ("Best eBike buying guide" not "click here")
- **Schema markup:** Add "BreadcrumbList" and "FAQPage" schema

## 💡 Monetization Strategy (When Eligible)

### Phase 1: X Premium Member Monetization (1K+ followers)
**Timeline:** Immediate when 1K threshold reached

**Implementation:**
- Enable Ads & Monetization in X Pro settings
- Set revenue goal: $500/month baseline
- Monitor dashboard weekly for performance

**Revenue Model:**
- 50% of ad revenue from Ads on posts (X keeps 50%)
- Depends on impressions, follower quality, engagement

### Phase 2: Brand Partnerships & Sponsorships (10K+ followers)
**Timeline:** After 1K, begin outreach at 5K followers

**Target Categories:**
- Sports betting platforms (DraftKings, FanDuel, etc.)
- Gaming equipment (hardware, streaming gear)
- Fantasy sports communities
- Sports media/news platforms

**Implementation Plan:**
1. **Pitch Package:** Create 1-pager with:
   - Audience demographics (sports fans, millennials, engaged)
   - Average impressions & engagement metrics
   - Sponsored post pricing ($200-500 range at 10K followers)
   - Posting schedule & exclusivity terms

2. **Outreach Campaign:**
   - Research 20 target brands (week 1)
   - Personalized pitches via email + Twitter DM (week 2-3)
   - Follow-up sequence (2x per brand, 1 week apart)
   - Terms: Net 30 payment, 2-4 week turnaround

3. **Content Guidelines:**
   - Disclose: Always add #ad or #sponsored tag (FTC requirement)
   - Authenticity: Only promote products Chip personally uses/believes in
   - Frequency: Max 1 sponsored post per day (maintain follower trust)

### Phase 3: Exclusive Media Partnerships (25K+ followers)
**Timeline:** Longer-term goal (Q3 2026+)

**Examples:**
- Exclusive content deals with sports media platforms
- Newsletter sponsorships (e.g., paid slots in sports newsletters)
- Affiliate programs (Amazon Associates, sports betting referral codes)

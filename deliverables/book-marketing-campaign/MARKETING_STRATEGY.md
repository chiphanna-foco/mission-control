# Gummy Bear Books - Marketing Campaign Strategy

**Book:** Gummy Bear Books (Children's Picture Book)  
**Author:** Teddy  
**Campaign Period:** 12 weeks pre-launch + ongoing  
**Budget:** $15,000-25,000 (detailed breakdown below)  
**Status:** Draft for Approval  

---

## Executive Summary

This marketing campaign is designed to:
1. **Generate awareness** among parents, educators, and gift-buyers
2. **Build pre-launch momentum** before book availability (email list, social following)
3. **Drive sales** through targeted advertising and influencer partnerships
4. **Establish author brand** as a trusted children's book author
5. **Create sustainable growth** through organic channels and community engagement

**Campaign Pillars:**
- Social media content (TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest)
- Amazon advertising (PPC, DSP)
- Influencer partnerships (BookTok creators, mommy bloggers)
- Blogger outreach (review copies, affiliate partnerships)
- Community engagement (schools, libraries, parent groups)

**Expected Results (90 days):**
- 5,000+ social media followers growth
- 1,000+ email subscribers
- 10,000+ impressions on Amazon ads
- 50+ book reviews (targeting 4.5+ rating)
- 500-1000 units sold in first 30 days

## Part 1: Social Media Strategy

### 1.1 Platform Selection & Priorities

**Primary Platform: TikTok**
- **Audience:** Gen Z (15-25) + millennial parents (25-45)
- **Why:** BookTok is massive for children's books; parents use TikTok to discover products
- **Content:** Behind-the-scenes, reading clips, author personality, trending sounds
- **Goals:** 5,000-10,000 followers, 100K+ monthly impressions
- **Investment:** 3 posts/week (15 min each = organic, no paid boost needed initially)

**Secondary Platform: Instagram**
- **Audience:** Millennial + Gen X parents (30-50)
- **Why:** Direct shopping, story ads, influencer collaborations
- **Content:** Book teasers, illustrations, parenting tips, reader testimonials
- **Goals:** 2,000-5,000 followers, product link in bio
- **Investment:** 4 posts/week (carousel, reels, stories)

**Tertiary Platform: Pinterest**
- **Audience:** Parents + educators (30-55) searching for children's books
- **Why:** High intent (save + buy), long content lifespan
- **Content:** Book covers, illustrated quotes, "best children's books" pins
- **Goals:** 1,000+ followers, 20+ viral pins (100K+ impressions each)
- **Investment:** 2 pins/day (minimal effort, use Canva templates)

**Emerging: YouTube Shorts**
- **Audience:** Young readers + parents
- **Content:** Reading clips, author Q&A, illustration process
- **Goals:** Cross-promote from TikTok, drive to blog
- **Investment:** Repurpose TikTok content (1 per week)

**Not Recommended Initially:** Facebook (lower ROI for children's books), LinkedIn (not relevant)

## Part 2: Amazon Advertising Strategy

### 2.1 Amazon Advertising Overview

**Goal:** Drive book sales through Amazon's advertising network  
**Timeline:** Start 4 weeks before launch, ramp up, sustain post-launch  
**Budget:** $6,000-10,000 (detailed below)  
**Expected ROI:** 3:1 to 5:1 (for every $1 spent, $3-5 in sales)

---

### 2.2 Campaign Types & Strategy

#### Campaign Type 1: Sponsored Products (Keyword-Targeted)
**Purpose:** Drive direct sales when customers search for books
**Strategy:** Bid on relevant keywords

**Keywords to Target:**
- Primary: "children's picture books", "gummy bear", "kids books ages 3-8"
- Secondary: "funny children's books", "colorful picture books", "best picture books"
- Long-tail: "books about friendship for kids", "adventure stories for children"

**Budget Allocation:** $4,000 (60% of total)

**Bid Strategy:**
- Manual CPC (Cost Per Click)
- Start bids at $0.50-$0.75 per keyword
- Adjust daily based on performance
- Target ACoS (Advertising Cost of Sale): 25-30%

**Expected Performance:**
- Daily spend: $50-80
- Monthly clicks: 2,000-3,000
- Monthly conversions: 100-150 books
- Click-through rate: 2-3%
- Conversion rate: 5-8%

---

#### Campaign Type 2: Sponsored Brands (Discovery)
**Purpose:** Build brand awareness, establish author credibility
**Strategy:** Promote author profile + "Explore all books by Teddy"

**Ad Creative:**
- Author photo + tagline
- 3-5 book cover images (if multiple books)
- Headline: "Discover Gummy Bear Books by Teddy"
- Call to action: "Shop Now"

**Budget Allocation:** $1,500 (20% of total)

**Bid Strategy:**
- Automatic targeting (Amazon finds relevant searches)
- Bid at $1.00-$2.00 per keyword
- Target ACoS: 35-40% (more brand-focused, lower ROI but builds awareness)

**Expected Performance:**
- Monthly clicks: 1,000-1,500
- Monthly conversions: 50-75 books
- Brand recall: Increase awareness among target audience

---

#### Campaign Type 3: Display Ads (Remarketing)
**Purpose:** Re-target website visitors, build frequency
**Strategy:** Show ads to people who visited Amazon but didn't buy

**Targeting:**
- Customers who viewed your product page but didn't buy
- Customers who viewed competitor books (similar titles)
- Customers who viewed parenting/children's book content

**Budget Allocation:** $500-1,000 (10% of total)

**Bid Strategy:**
- CPC: $0.20-$0.50 (lower cost for re-targeting)
- Target ACoS: 20-25% (high conversion from interested audience)

---

#### Campaign Type 4: Amazon DSP (Demand Side Platform)
**Purpose:** Build brand awareness across web/apps
**Strategy:** Display ads on partner sites, other apps, Twitch

**Audience:**
- Parents interested in children's books
- Parenting content browsers
- Amazon shoppers (demographics: parents, 25-55)

**Budget Allocation:** $1,000 (if budget allows)

**Bid Strategy:**
- CPM (Cost Per Thousand Impressions): $3-8
- Target 100K+ impressions
- Focus on awareness, not direct conversions

**Weekly Review (Every Monday):**
- [ ] Check ACoS (Advertising Cost of Sale)
- [ ] Review clicks, impressions, conversions
- [ ] Identify top-performing keywords
- [ ] Pause underperforming keywords/ads
- [ ] Adjust bids for high-performers
- [ ] Check daily budget burn (pace vs. budget)

**Daily Monitoring (Quick Checks):**
- [ ] Ensure campaigns are live

- [ ] Monitor spend (vs. daily budget)

**Monthly Optimization (End of Month):**
- [ ] Full campaign performance review
- [ ] Negative keyword analysis (add non-converting terms)
- [ ] Budget reallocation based on performance
- [ ] Keyword expansion (add new related terms)
- [ ] A/B test new ad copy/images

## Part 3: Influencer & Blogger Outreach

### 3.1 Influencer Categories

**Category 1: BookTok Creators (High Priority)**
- **Audience:** 100K-1M followers interested in books
- **Engagement:** Very high (5-15%)
- **Cost:** $500-2,000 per video (or free books for <100K followers)
- **Examples:** Pooja Reads, Vivian Book Confessions, NovelNetflix
- **Target:** 5-10 creators

**Strategy:**
- Send review copy + personalized note
- Ask for honest review (don't mandate positive)
- Offer affiliate link (if they mention)
- Follow up 2 weeks after sending

**Expected Impact:**
- Per creator: 50K-500K impressions
- Per creator: 10-50 book sales
- Total reach: 500K+ impressions, 100-250 sales

---

**Category 2: Mommy Bloggers (Medium Priority)**
- **Audience:** 5K-100K followers (parents, educators)
- **Engagement:** 2-5%
- **Cost:** Free (review copy) or $100-300 (sponsored post)
- **Examples:** Mommy Blogger Network, Parent Bloggers, Kids Activities Blog
- **Target:** 10-15 bloggers

**Strategy:**
- Identify 15 relevant mommy blogs (search: "best children's books" blog)
- Personalized email pitch
- Offer review copy + affiliate link
- Guest post opportunity (author tips for parents)
- Expected: 30% response rate, 5-10 reviews

**Expected Impact:**
- Per blogger: 5K-50K impressions
- Per blogger: 5-20 book sales
- Total reach: 100K+ impressions, 75-200 sales
- SEO benefit: Links back to Amazon listing

---

**Category 3: Children's Book Reviewers (High Quality)**
- **Audience:** 1K-50K followers (dedicated book readers)
- **Engagement:** 3-8%
- **Cost:** Free (review copy)
- **Examples:** Read Madly, Bookworm Boutique, Kids Book Review
- **Target:** 20-30 reviewers

**Strategy:**
- Research relevant book reviewers on Goodreads, Instagram, YouTube
- Personalized email with book summary
- Offer early access (pre-publication)
- Ask for honest reviews on Goodreads + Amazon
- Expected: 40-50% response rate, 10-15 reviews

**Expected Impact:**
- Per reviewer: 1K-10K impressions
- Per reviewer: 5-15 book sales
- Total reach: 50K+ impressions, 100-200 sales
- Critical: Goodreads reviews build credibility

---

**Category 4: Educators (Long-term, Phase 2)**
- **Audience:** 100-500 students per teacher
- **Engagement:** Very high (captive audience)
- **Cost:** Classroom sets or subsidized prices
- **Examples:** Teacher Instagram accounts, EdTech influencers
- **Target:** 5-10 teachers (Phase 2)

**Strategy:**
- Wait until book is published
- Offer classroom sets at discount (40-50% off)
- Provide discussion guides for teachers
- Ask for classroom reviews/testimonials
- Expected: 50% conversion to classroom adoption

## Channel Breakdown

### 1. Social Media Strategy ($2,000)

**Primary Platform: TikTok** (5,000+ followers target)
- Post 3-5 times/week
- Focus: Behind-the-scenes, book teasers, author personality
- Budget: $500 for content tools + paid promotion
- Expected reach: 100K+ monthly impressions

**Secondary: Instagram** (3,000+ followers target)
- Post 4-5 times/week
- Focus: Educational content, reader testimonials, parent tips
- Budget: $500 for paid ads + content creation
- Expected reach: 50K+ monthly impressions

**Tertiary: Pinterest** (1,000+ followers target)
- Post 2 pins/day
- Focus: Book covers, quotes, "best books" lists
- Budget: $200 for design + minimal paid (mostly organic)
- Expected reach: 500K+ monthly impressions (long-tail)

**Email List Building:**
- Landing page signup
- Incentive: Free reading guide + coloring pages
- Target: 1,000+ subscribers by launch
- Budget: $300 for email platform setup

---

### 2. Amazon Advertising Strategy ($8,000)

**Campaign Types:**

1. **Sponsored Products** ($4,000 / 60% budget)
   - Keyword targeting: "children's books", "picture books", "gummy bear"
   - Daily spend: $50-80
   - Expected: 2,000-3,000 clicks/month, 100-150 sales/month
   - Target ACoS (cost of sale): 25-30%

2. **Sponsored Brands** ($1,500 / 20% budget)
   - Brand awareness campaign
   - Daily spend: $25-40
   - Expected: 1,000-1,500 clicks/month, 50-75 sales/month
   - Target ACoS: 35-40%

3. **Display Ads + DSP** ($1,500 / 20% budget)
   - Remarketing to website visitors
   - Brand awareness across Amazon ecosystem
   - Expected: 100-150 sales/month
   - Target ACoS: 20-25%

4. **Reserve Fund** ($1,000)
   - Test new keyword opportunities
   - Handle unexpected budget needs
   - Optimization buffer

**Weekly Management:**
- Monitor ACoS (Advertising Cost of Sale)
- Pause underperforming keywords
- Adjust bids for top performers
- Compile performance reports

2. **Mommy Bloggers** (Tier B)

**Expected Results:**

## 🎒 School Visits & Educational Programs

### About School Visits

Teddy offers engaging, interactive author visits perfect for schools and educational institutions. Students meet the author, learn about the creative process, and discover what it takes to become a published author.

### What's Included

**Standard 45-Minute School Visit:**
- ✓ Interactive author talk (20 minutes)
- ✓ Book reading & storytelling (15 minutes)
- ✓ Q&A with students (10 minutes)
- ✓ Book signing/personalization (if desired)
- ✓ Photo opportunity
- ✓ Discussion of writing & illustration process

**Extended Program (60-90 minutes):**
- ✓ Everything above, PLUS:
- ✓ Interactive writing/illustration workshop
- ✓ Behind-the-scenes creative demo
- ✓ Extended Q&A and discussion

### Grade Levels & Customization
- **Grades K-2:** Picture book focus, interactive storytelling
- **Grades 3-5:** Writing process, character development workshop
- **Grades 6-8:** Advanced writing techniques, author career insights
- **Middle & High School:** Creative writing mentorship, publishing industry overview

### School Visit Booking

**To schedule a school visit:**
1. **Contact:** schools@teddyhanna.com
2. **Include:** School name, grade level, preferred dates, visit type
3. **Timeline:** Book 4-6 weeks in advance when possible
4. **Cost:** [Fee structure — e.g., $[X] per visit, or [X] per student]
5. **Travel:** Schools within [X] miles receive [discount/free shipping of travel costs]

### Pre-Visit Materials
Schools receive:
- ✓ Discussion guides
- ✓ Activity worksheets
- ✓ Teacher's notes
- ✓ Vocabulary lists (if applicable)
- ✓ Background on author and books

### Post-Visit Follow-Up
- ✓ Thank you note from Teddy
- ✓ Signed classroom certificate
- ✓ Digital resources for continued learning
- ✓ Discounted classroom book sets available

## 🎯 FAQs About Events

**Q: Is there a cost to attend events?**
A: Most events are free! Some workshops have a small fee ($[X]-$[XX]). Virtual events are often free or donation-based. Check the specific event for details.

**Q: Can I bring my children to adult events?**
A: Most events welcome families! Family-friendly events are noted as such. For other events, contact us to ask.

**Q: Can my school book an author visit?**
A: Yes! Schools are encouraged to book visits. Email schools@teddyhanna.com with your school name, grade levels, and preferred dates. Advance notice of 4-6 weeks is appreciated.

**Q: Is Teddy available for private events?**
A: Yes, private events, corporate events, and special appearances can be arranged. Email events@teddyhanna.com with details.

**Q: What if I can't make an in-person event?**
A: Many events are available virtually! Check the event listing for Zoom or online options.

**Q: How do I get updates about future events?**
A: [Subscribe to our newsletter](link) to receive event announcements in your inbox.

**Q: Can I meet Teddy?**
A: Yes! At most events, there's time for photos, book signings, and brief conversations with the author. Book signings are a great time to chat!

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the best place to buy?**
A: All formats are readily available. Choose based on format preference (print, eBook, audiobook) and where you prefer to shop (Amazon for convenience, Bookshop.org to support local stores, your library for free access).

**Q: Are the books available as audiobooks?**
A: Yes! All titles are available on Audible, Apple Books, and Google Play. Check your library app for free access through Libby or Hoopla.

**Q: Can I get a signed copy?**
A: Yes! Order signed copies directly from our site or meet Teddy at a book signing event. See our [Events page](link) for upcoming appearances.

**Q: Do you offer bulk discounts?**
A: Yes! Schools, libraries, and organizations qualify for special bulk pricing. Email bulk@teddyhanna.com for details.

**Q: How can I order outside the US?**
A: International versions are available through Amazon UK, Bol.com, and local retailers in your country. Check the [International Retailers](link) section above.

**Q: What if I can't find a book?**
A: Try these options:
1. Ask your local library to order it
2. Contact your local bookstore and request they stock it
3. Order directly online from Amazon or Bookshop.org
4. Email us at hello@teddyhanna.com for ordering assistance

## 💡 Key Content Additions

### 1. Community Service Section
**Add:**
- Recent workshops (schools, count of students)
- Pageant platform usage
- Nonprofit partnerships
- Impact by numbers (students reached, units distributed)

**Example:**
```
"In 2025-2026, Scarlett has taught 400+ students proper 
handwashing across 10 Colorado schools."
```

### 2. Product Section (NEW)
**Add:**
- Product features
- Where to buy (online + retail)
- Pricing
- Bulk order info

### 3. Enhanced Testimonials
**Add:**
- Student quotes
- Teacher/principal endorsements
- Parent testimonials
- Organization quotes

### 4. "Book Scarlett" Details
**Add:**
- What's included (5-min vs 15-min options)
- Recent bookings/events
- How to request
- Pricing
- Availability

### 5. SEO Improvements
**Add:**
- Meta title: Include target keywords
- Meta description: 155 chars, compelling
- Schema.org markup (LocalBusiness, Product, Event)
- H1/H2 hierarchy
- Image alt text
- Internal linking

## 📞 Quick Questions to Answer

**Before you start:**

1. **Current Status**
   - What is Scarlett's current pageant title?

   - Any recent achievements or awards?

2. **Product**

   - Any recent professional photos available?

## 💡 COST-SAVING STRATEGIES

1. **Borrow Formal Wear:** Ask friends/family if you can borrow evening gown or cocktail dresses
2. **DIY Hair & Makeup:** Learn YouTube tutorials; only hire professional for pageant day
3. **Budget Airline:** Use Spirit/Frontier for savings ($150-300 per ticket)
4. **Roommate Sharing:** Share hotel room with another contestant (split cost)
5. **Meal Planning:** Eat hotel breakfast, pack snacks, limit restaurant visits
6. **Early Bird Discounts:** Register early for pageants (sometimes 10-20% discount)
7. **Local Talent:** Use local photographer for pageant photos (cheaper than professional pageant photographer)
8. **Wardrobe Reuse:** Wear same cocktail dress for multiple events
9. **Transportation:** Ride with other contestants to/from airport and activities
10. **Group Rates:** Inquire about group travel packages with Cinderella

**Potential Savings: $1,000-1,500**

## 📈 FUNDING OPTIONS

If budget is tight, consider:

1. **Pageant Scholarships:** Cinderella awards scholarships - every competitor can earn back funds
2. **Local Sponsorships:** Approach local businesses for pageant sponsorship
3. **Family Contributions:** Ask family to sponsor specific costs (flights, hotel, registration)
4. **Job/Part-time Income:** Work extra hours April-July to save funds
5. **Payment Plans:** Some pageants offer payment plans instead of lump sum
6. **Crowdfunding:** Set up GoFundMe for pageant season
7. **Scholarship Winnings:** Each pageant awards scholarships that can fund future events
